 Introducing Relational Databases.

 1) Which field of the Customers table is the primary key? 

Ans: The primary key is  the CNUM field (Customer Number), as it uniquely identifies each customer in the table.

2) What is the 4th column of the Customers table? 

Ans: The 4th column is RATING.


3) What is another word for row?  For column? 

Ans: Another word for a row is record or tuple.
     Another word for a column is field or attribute.


4) Why isn’t it possible to see the first five rows of a table? 

Ans:   the data is visible here, there’s no issue in this case. However, if you couldn't see the first five rows, it might be due to:
1. Permissions: Lack of access to the table.
2. Query issue: Incorrect query to fetch the rows.
3. No data: The table could be empty.
4. View settings: The display might be limited.
